ABOUT US

Fishcraft is a 21+ Minecraft server with the goal to create a fun and relaxed getaway for the community. We are a fun and mature bunch and we are looking for like-minded people to hang out with.

We use Discord as our primary form of communication. All of the information about important announcements, server events, and fun banter happens there!

Frequently Asked Questions

+
We currently have a lightly Modded SMP server, which is relatively new with a lot of land yet to be explored. The main mods include Create 0.5, Biomes You'll Go, Farmer's Delight and Chipped.

We have also hosted a long-running vanilla SMP for the past couple of years and might start up a new one if there's sufficient interest.
+
We are a whitelisted server so to acquire access to it you have to apply via application form under faq, MinecraftForums or Subreddit threads we post weekly.
+
We are currently playing in 1.18.2 in the modded server. We do not have plans to upgrade the world itself but might consider updating when all the mods are available for 1.20.
+
There is no cost to join and although donations are appreciated, they are in no way mandatory or necessary.

If you are enjoying your time in the server and would like to support us, please check out our Donation Page. All donations are used to help cover for server hosting costs.
+
We definitely welcome feedback and suggestions from our members to improve your experience! In our current modded world we are trying to keep it as light as possible to keep it accessible to a wider player base.
+
The server is hosted in US. We have players from around the globe and have had no issues/latency reported.
+
We can accept 20yr old applicatns but it will depend on your application form as well
