ABOUT US

Fishcraft is a 21 and over SMP community with two minecraft servers, one vanilla(Offline atm) and a new one Modded server. We are a fun mature community and we are looking for like-minded people to hang out with. The server/map is really new so there's a lot to explore, build and create. I hope you can join us and have a good time playing minecraft with us. We use Discord as our primary form of communication. All of the information about important announcements, server events, and fun banter happens there!
